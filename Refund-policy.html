Refund Policy

Effective Date: [25 March 2025]

Thank you for shopping with GamsWorld. We value your satisfaction and aim to provide a fair and transparent refund policy.

1. Digital Products

All digital products, including downloadable content and games, are non-refundable once the purchase is completed and the product is delivered.

2. Subscription Services

If you are not satisfied with your subscription, you may request a refund within 7 days of purchase. Refunds will be issued only if no content has been accessed or downloaded during the period.

3. Duplicate Payments

If you have been charged more than once for the same transaction, please contact us immediately. We will verify and initiate a refund within 7 working days.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at support@gamsworld.online with your order ID and reason for the refund.

5. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed to the original method of payment within 7-10 business days.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us at support@gamsworld.online.
